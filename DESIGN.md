# Website Style Guide: Johanna Lundahl Advocacy & Research

This document outlines the visual identity, typography, and content structure for a professional website dedicated to the work of Johanna Lundahl, focusing on Seattle-based community organizing, labor rights, and poverty reduction research.

---

## 1. Brand Identity
**Core Mission:** "Nothing About Us Without Us"
**Tone:** Grounded, Persistent, Professional, and Community-Centric.

---

## 2. Color Palette
The colors are inspired by the Pacific Northwest landscape and urban Seattle architecture.

| Element | Color Name | Hex Code | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary** | Deep Spruce | `#2D4F4F` | Branding, Headers, Navigation |
| **Secondary** | Slate Grey | `#708090` | Sub-headers, Accents, Dividers |
| **Accent** | Terracotta | `#E2725B` | **Call to Action Buttons**, Links |
| **Background**| Oatmeal | `#F9F7F2` | Page Background (Warm neutral) |
| **Text** | Charcoal | `#333333` | Body Copy (High legibility) |

---

## 3. Typography
A pairing of a modern geometric sans-serif for impact and a classic serif for research-heavy readability.

### **Headings (Montserrat)**
* **H1 (Page Title):** `48px` | Bold | `#2D4F4F`
* **H2 (Section):** `32px` | Semi-Bold | `#2D4F4F`
* **H3 (Sub-header):** `24px` | Medium | `#708090`

### **Body (Lora)**
* **Paragraphs:** `18px` | Regular | Line Height: `1.6`
* **Captions:** `14px` | Italic | `#708090`

---

## 4. UI Components

### **Buttons**
* **Style:** Rounded corners (`4px`).
* **Color:** Background `#E2725B` | Text `#F9F7F2`.
* **Hover:** Background `#D15F49`.
* **Usage:** Limit this color to "Join," "Donate," or "Read Report" to maintain visual priority.

### **Content Cards**
* **Border:** `1px` solid `#708090`.
* **Shadow:** `0 4px 6px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`.
* **Purpose:** Use for the "Troublemakers Community" event feed or blog updates.

---

## 5. Site Architecture

### **I. Home (The Frontline)**
* Hero image of Seattle community organizing.
* Latest updates from **Federal Building Fridays**.

### **II. Research & Policy**
* Downloadable reports on Washington State poverty dismantling.
* Summaries of the **Nothing About Us Without Us Act**.

### **III. Archive & Storytelling**
* Links to early journalism and photography at [The Daily UW](https://www.dailyuw.com/staff/johanna-lundahl).
* Essays on food sovereignty and global justice.

### **IV. Contact/Get Involved**
* Form for community collaboration and speaking engagements.

---

## 6. Technical Specifications
* **Platform:** Squarespace, Webflow, or Static Site (Hugo/Jekyll).
* **Accessibility:** Must meet **WCAG 2.1 AA** standards (ensure `#E2725B` text has enough contrast on `#F9F7F2`).
* **Imagery:** Use natural, unposed photography with a slight "cool" Seattle-toned filter.
